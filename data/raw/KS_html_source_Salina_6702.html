What Is L2Q?

Links to Quality (L2Q) is a new voluntary program in Kansas that focuses on improving early child care and educational environments. L2Q helps child care educators build on strengths in business practices, family partnerships, health and safety, and learning and child development through earning Link badges. The badges earned represent the hard work that goes into each area and helps families choose the child care and education program that is the best fit for their children. Not all providers will have earned badges yet because it takes time to achieve one.

Program Administration
The Program Administration Link celebrates programs with high-quality business practices. An early childhood program has clear policies, wise budget skills, and aims for a supportive work setting..

Family Partnership
Family Partnership Link shows that early childhood programs work hard to build strong connections with families and the community. Programs work with families for the goals and needs of the child.

Health and Safety
Health and Safety Link fosters safe conditions and a healthy approach to care. Early childhood programs have a risk management plan, share information about healthy practices, and make space for active play.

Learning and Development
Learning and Development Link shows that programs create fun and positive places to learn and build relationships. Early childhood programs create learning experiences and engage children with each other and educators.
